Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

This Chrome extension ("Stablescan") does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information about its users.

Information Usage

The extension only accesses stablecoin-related news data from trusted external sources (e.g., APIs or public news websites) in order to provide users with up-to-date information.

No personal data, browsing history, or user activity is tracked, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

The extension may request certain Chrome permissions (such as storage, alarms, or tabs) only to provide its core functionality. These permissions are not used to collect user data.
